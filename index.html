Frequently Asked Questions

Why create another task tracker when there are already so many?

We’ve tried countless popular trackers ourselves, but none felt quite right. So we decided to build something different — a tool we’d genuinely enjoy using every single day.

What’s special about your app?

It’s all about simplicity and purpose. We cleared away the clutter and kept only what truly helps you manage tasks effectively. At the same time, we added thoughtful touches you won’t find in many other apps — like intermediate statuses and a daily review habit that keeps you connected to your work.

What is the review process?

It’s a small but life-changing habit: taking a moment each day — morning or evening — to look through all your tasks. This simple practice brings clarity. You’ll find ways to refine, split, or even remove tasks you no longer need. More importantly, daily reviews help you stay on top of everything, giving you a sense of calm even during your busiest times. It’s a little act of self-care for your mind.

What are intermediate statuses?

Sometimes ticking off a task as "done" doesn’t tell the whole story. Maybe you’ve handed a task to a teammate and need to follow up later. In #Backlog, you can easily move tasks into an "Under Control" status — so tomorrow, when you do your review, you’ll instantly know which ones need a check-in. No more forgotten conversations or loose ends.

Why is the app paid, and why by subscription?

The honest truth: we're not here to make a fortune. We simply want to keep the app clean — no ads, no hidden catches. Even in the free version, you can manage a huge number of tasks. If you ever find yourself reaching the free limit, it means our app is truly helping you get things done — and that’s a beautiful thing. A small subscription helps keep #Backlog alive and growing, and it shows us that what we built is really making a difference. And that support means more to us than you can imagine.
